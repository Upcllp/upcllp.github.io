Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long will it take to receive my order?

    Majority of our orders are handed over to our logistics partners within 24 hours of your order. For custom printed order take an additional 3 days. 95 % of our orders are delivered within 2 days after shipment.

  • What is your price for bulk quantity?

    Please e-mail your request to sales@upackaging.in and we will be happy to provide a bulk quotation for packaging materials, customised boxes, carton boxes, packing tapes, bubble roll, stretch film, etc.

  • How can I trust ordering from you?

    Universal Packaging is GST registered company in business since 2021 with several happy customers. Take a look at our real customer reviews.

  • Is it possible to get samples?

    We are happy to provide samples of any types of packaging, product packaging boxes and other packing material whenever possible. Please e-mail us at support@upackaging.in and we will check to see if we have sample stock available. You can also order samples online using the link in the footer.

  • Can I receive copy of my invoice?

    We send your invoice along with the shipment. If you need another copy, simply e-mail us at sales@upackagig.in and we will e-mail you a copy
